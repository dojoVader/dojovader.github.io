It has been a while updating this journal, I have found myself dealing with work and lethargy mostly as a result of nothing exciting to do at the moment. But today I decided to try out Mobile solutions. The last time I wrote for mobile was via Apache Cordova, which is a project built on the WebView component of both the Android and IOS Platform, it allows JavaScript to communicate to the respective Native API. 

Now fast-forward the options are much diverse than ever. There is NativeScript, ReactNative , Flutter and also Android with new Architectures making it easier to write modern codes now. Not to forget the JVM Language Kotlin.

This seems like an exciting time, However I do have a current dilemma at hand. One of the pitfall of Hybrid Applications is not having raw and ultimate power to do every and anything but also the additional absctraction. Apache Cordova required me to look at the source code so I could understand the underlying architecture better and gain some insights. This knowledge was helpful when dealing with Cordova Plugin Development. 

My Approach are :

1. Pick up Hybrid then later on Android Native Development

2. Pick up Native Android before diving into Hybrid

3. Unofficial option

Pick up both side by side.


Watch our for this space, as I will document my learning and progress. Be Cool and stayed tuned. I hope to update this at least every week.
